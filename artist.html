The Artist

Gene Chu is a 'printmaker's printmaker'. Essentially this means that his technical virtuosity and fashion-resistant work has been widely recognized by his fellow artists. From the outset over thirty years ago Gene Chu has created by means of the various printmaking techniques a very substantial body of imagery concerned with the central themes of our existence: the politics of experience and morality. These concerns link him with the great humanistic and graphic traditions of western and eastern culture.
